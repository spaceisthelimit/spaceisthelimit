Donations

If you are interested in assisting AHAN in our efforts to protect the planet and its precious animals, please consider a donation. If you or someone you know would like to volunteer services, items, or be put on our mailing list, please email us at: contact@ahan.org

Donations by Mail: Angels for Humans, Animals & Nature 3738 Balboa Street Suite 233 San Francisco , CA 94121

By doing this you will:

  • Avoid expensive repair and upkeep for the car
  • Receive a higher market value for tax reduction purposes.
  • You will be doing something very meaningful by helping us continue helping animals and nature

AHAN.org is a San Francisco Bay Area International animal rescue organization dedicated to the rescue of abandoned and neglected overseas' dogs and cats, fostering, and adoptions.
